Tafseer, tumhare aane se meri zindagi mein wo rang aaye hain jo maine kabhi khuwab mein bhi nahi dekhe thay. Main, Syed Zulfiqar Mehdi Naqvi, aaj tum se wo kehna chahta hoon jo lafzon mein kehna mushkil hai.

Tum meri zindagi ki wo kitab ho jis ka har panna mohabbat se bhara hai. Main chahta hoon ke hamari ye dastan hamesha ke liye amar ho jaye. Jab tak meri saansein baaqi hain, main sirf tumhara rahunga.
Main tumhe wo sari khushiyan dena chahta hoon jis ki tum haqdaar ho. Tum sirf mera ishq nahi, tum meri izzat aur mera maan ho. Kya tum tayyar ho meri har dua ka hissa banne ke liye?
